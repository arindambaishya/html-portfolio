The Best Movies According to Arindam

My top 3 movies of all-time.

The Shawshank Redemption(1994 ‧ Crime/Drama ‧ 2h 22m)

Andy Dufresne, a successful banker, is arrested for the murders of his wife and her lover, and is sentenced to life imprisonment at the Shawshank prison. He becomes the most unconventional prisoner.

The Revenant(2015 ‧ Western/Adventure ‧ 2h 36m)

Hugh Glass, a legendary frontiersman, is severely injured in a bear attack and is abandoned by his hunting crew. He uses his skills to survive and take revenge on his companion, who betrayed him.

Training Day(2001 ‧ Thriller/Crime ‧ 2h 2m)

When South Central Los Angeles sees the rise of drug dealers, Jake Hoyt is trained by Alonzo Harries of L.A.P.D to knock down the bad guys. But soon, his training methods draw objections from others.
